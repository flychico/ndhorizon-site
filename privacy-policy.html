Privacy Policy

These terms are written in plain language so visitors understand how ND Horizon handles information, website use, and consulting limitations.

Effective date: January 1, 2026

ND Horizon respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected through this website and how that information may be used.

Information we collect

When you contact ND Horizon through the website, email, or a scheduling link, we may collect information you choose to provide, such as your name, business name, email address, phone number, and details about your business needs.

Website analytics

This website may use analytics tools, including Google Analytics, to understand traffic, page activity, and visitor behavior in aggregate. This helps improve the website, content, and user experience.

How information is used

  • To respond to inquiries and consultation requests.
  • To schedule calls and communicate about potential services.
  • To improve website content, structure, and performance.
  • To maintain business records and comply with applicable obligations.

Information sharing

ND Horizon does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the business, respond to your request, use service providers, or comply with legal obligations.

Third-party links

This website may link to third-party tools such as scheduling platforms, email services, analytics tools, or other resources. Those services operate under their own privacy practices.

Your choices

You may contact ND Horizon to request that your contact information be updated or removed from active communication lists, subject to any records ND Horizon needs to keep for legitimate business or legal purposes.

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to lynold@ndhorizon.com.
